PoE Website Item Copy

  • Go to Path of Exile website.
  • Click on your character name to access his inventory/stash.
  • Hover the item you want to copy then press C (not ctrl).
  • A message should indicates the item got copied to the clipboard, you can now paste it in Path of Building.
